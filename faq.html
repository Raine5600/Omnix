Website & Services

Your website is custom built for your business — not a generic template. We design it around your brand, services, and target customers to make sure it converts visitors into leads. Every site we build is unique to the business it represents.

Your website is hosted and maintained as part of your Omnix subscription. If you ever choose to leave, we can discuss transferring the site assets to you. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation and we'll work out the best arrangement.

After a job is completed, your customers automatically receive a follow-up message (SMS or email) asking them to rate their experience. Happy customers are guided directly to leave a Google review, while unsatisfied customers are directed to a private feedback form so you can resolve issues before they become public. This creates a steady stream of 5-star reviews on autopilot.

When someone calls your business and you can't answer, they automatically receive a text message within seconds letting them know you'll be in touch soon. This keeps potential customers engaged instead of calling your competitor, and can dramatically reduce missed business opportunities.

Local SEO (Search Engine Optimization) ensures your business shows up when people in your area search for the services you offer. We optimize your Google Business Profile, build local citations, and keep your information consistent across the web — so you rank higher and get found by more local customers.
