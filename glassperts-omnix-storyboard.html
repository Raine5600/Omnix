Omnix Prompt — Submission Handling

Prompt

When the user clicks SEND, run this logic:

1. Validate that Full Name, Phone, and Message are filled AND checkbox is checked. Show inline red error messages if not.

2. On valid submit: show a success confirmation inside the card — replace the form with a message like: "Thank you! We'll be in touch shortly." with a gold checkmark icon.

3. Simultaneously trigger a backend webhook / SMS automation with the submitted lead data: name, phone number, and message.

4. Log the lead to a database or CRM entry for the business owner to review.

  • Validation: Required fields highlight red if empty on submit attempt
  • Checkbox required: Must be checked — show error if unchecked
  • Success state: Form replaced with thank-you message, gold ✓ icon, warm confirmation copy
  • Backend trigger: POST request fires to webhook (Twilio / SMS provider) with lead data
  • Lead data captured: Full Name, Phone Number, Message, Timestamp, Source URL

Omnix Prompt — SMS Text Alert to Business Owner

Prompt

When a quote form is submitted, instantly send an SMS text message to the business owner's phone number using a messaging service (e.g. Twilio or similar).

The text message should read:

"🔔 New Quote Request!
Name: [Full Name]
Phone: [Phone Number]
Message: [Their message]
Time: [Timestamp]
Source: TheGlassperts.com"

This allows the business owner to see the lead notification right on their phone, even if they're not at a computer. The text arrives within seconds of form submission.

  • SMS provider: Twilio (recommended), or any webhook-to-SMS service
  • Recipient: Business owner's mobile number (set in backend config)
  • Content: Includes all submitted fields — name, phone, message, timestamp
  • Speed: Delivers within 3–10 seconds of form submission
  • Also optional: Send confirmation SMS to the customer's number as well
  • Fallback: Email notification also sent as backup

Complete Omnix Prompt

Build a contractor website for a local glass repair business called "The Glassperts". The site should include:

1. HEADER: Sticky charcoal nav bar with the Glassperts hexagon logo, nav links (Home, Services▾, Gallery, Service Areas▾, Blog, Contact), a gold "GET FREE QUOTE" button that scrolls to the form, and a phone button showing (866)-483-7546.

2. HERO: Full-viewport section with a background photo of open sliding glass doors (ocean view). Left side has a large bold white headline: "THE GLASSPERTS SLIDING GLASS DOOR & WINDOW REPAIR" plus a short body paragraph about 5+ years experience.

3. QUOTE FORM CARD: A floating dark navy card on the right side of the hero containing: the logo, "GET A FREE QUOTE" heading, two side-by-side inputs (Full Name, Phone), a full-width textarea (Short message about your needs), a terms & conditions checkbox with SMS consent language, and a full-width gold SEND button.

4. AUTOMATION: When the form is submitted, validate all required fields, show a success confirmation, and instantly send an SMS text message to the business owner's phone with the lead's name, phone number, message, and timestamp using Twilio.

5. CHAT WIDGET: Fixed teal circular chat button in the bottom-right corner of every page.

COLOR PALETTE: Navy #1A2340 | Gold #F5A623 | Charcoal #2C2C2C | Teal #2AB5C4 | White text.
