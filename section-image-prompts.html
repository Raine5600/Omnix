Section Feature Image Prompts

One Grok image prompt per feature section. Generate each image and drop it into the matching placeholder on the Omnix website.

Missed Call Text Back

Someone calls and can't reach the business — an automatic text fires back instantly. Show the phone interaction and the instant reply.

Section 3 on Homepage
Grok Image Prompt — Copy & Paste
COPY →
Photorealistic split-scene image: On the left side, a contractor or tradesperson in a work shirt is on a job site, hands busy working, their phone face-down on a surface showing a missed call notification. On the right side, a close-up of a customer's phone screen lighting up with an incoming text message that reads: "Hi! Sorry we missed your call — we'd love to help. Reply here or visit our site to book." The customer's hand is visible picking up the phone, looking pleased. Cinematic lighting, professional lifestyle photography, dark and moody left side contrasting with warm glowing right side, 4K, photorealistic.
Split compositionTwo halves tell the whole story — busy contractor misses call, customer instantly gets a text back. No explanation needed.
Lighting contrastDark/cool left side (missed call) vs. warm/bright right side (text reply) creates visual storytelling.
AlternativeIf Grok struggles with split-scene, just generate the customer's phone lighting up with the auto-text — that's the money shot.
Negative Prompt

cartoon, illustration, angry customer, voicemail screen, office setting, suit and tie, blurry text, unreadable message, distorted phone

Photorealistic close-up of a contractor's rough, work-worn hand holding a smartphone.
